Streak Search

An automatic search for "killstreak" on the Steam Marketplace that allows you to filter results so you only see kits, fabricators, weapons of a certain quality, etc. The data is scraped from Valve every 10 minutes.

Fake Item Generator

Have an idea for a new item? Or an idea to rebalance an existing one? This generator will let you create a mockup for what the item's stats would be, allowing you to set the image as well as its positive, negative, and neutral attributes. Make your (bad) idea stand out when you post about it somewhere!
